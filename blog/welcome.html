Welcome to My Research Blog

May 23, 2026 · Announcement

Welcome to my personal website and research blog. I'm a 3rd Year PhD Student in Biomedical Informatics at Vanderbilt University, and I'll be using this space to share updates on my research, thoughts on topics in AI and healthcare, and reflections on the PhD journey.

My research focuses on three main areas:

  • Synthetic data generation — Creating privacy-preserving synthetic medical datasets using generative AI, enabling broader access to high-quality data for research.
  • Federated learning — Developing collaborative machine learning methods that enable institutions to train models across distributed data without sharing sensitive patient information.
  • Algorithmic fairness — Investigating approaches to mitigate bias and ensure equitable performance of medical AI systems across diverse populations.

I'm excited to have a space where I can write about ongoing projects, share lessons learned, and engage with the broader research community. Check back for new posts!
