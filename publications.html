Research & Publications

My research has had three main directions: privacy-preserving data-sharing, generalizable AI for healthcare, and human-AI interaction. My dissertation work will focus on the intersection of the first two.

Research Directions

🔒

Privacy-Preserving Data Sharing

Developing generative AI methods to create high-quality synthetic medical data that preserves privacy while maintaining key scientific properties and utility for AI training.

⚖️

Algorithmic Fairness / Generalizability

Investigating methods to mitigate bias and ensure generalizability of medical AI across populations.

🤝

Human-AI Interaction

Studying how AI systems interact with and support human decision-making in clinical contexts.
